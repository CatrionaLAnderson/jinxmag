About Us

Welcome to Jinx Mag, our culture and lifestyle magazine where we explore fashion, music, art, and anything else that takes our fancy.
We’re three university students living in London, balancing our final year with this creative project that started as a small idea and has grown into something we’re really proud of.
Our team brings a mix of backgrounds and perspectives: Grace and Ciara are from Kent, while Cat is from Scotland. Between us, we study a range of subjects, from humanities to science, so we have a weird but great mix of ideas. What brings us together is a shared intrest for art, music, fashion, and thought-provoking topics like politics and other controversial issues. We have a lot of opinions (seriously, so many), and Jinx Mag is our space to share them and invite others into the conversation. This zine is about more than just self-expression; it’s about learning from the process, collaborating with amazing people, and, most importantly, having fun along the way. We hope you enjoy exploring Jinx Mag as much as we’ve enjoyed creating it!

This is only just the beginning of our magazine and we are really excited to learn more as this journey continues.
